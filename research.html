Research

Catalysis

Exploring catalytic reaction mechanisms using density functional theory (DFT) calculations. My work focuses on understanding how ligand environments influence catalytic activity and reaction pathways, particularly in metal–NHC systems involved in CO₂ activation and insertion processes.

Excited State Dynamics

Investigating photophysical behaviour of molecular systems using computational electronic structure methods. These studies focus on excited-state properties, charge transfer processes and the theoretical understanding of thermally activated delayed fluorescence (TADF) emitters.

Machine Learning in Chemistry

Applying machine learning approaches to interpret computational data and reveal structure–property relationships in catalytic systems. Data-driven models help simplify complex chemical datasets and provide deeper insight into reaction trends and catalyst design.
