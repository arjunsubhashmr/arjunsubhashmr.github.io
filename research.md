# Research

## Cu(I)–NHC Complexes for CO₂ Insertion

Design and evaluation of Cu(I)–NHC complexes using Density Functional Theory and machine learning guided descriptor analysis.

## TD-DFT Investigation of TADF Emitters

Benchmarking density functionals to study singlet–triplet gaps, oscillator strengths and charge transfer properties in donor–acceptor emitters.

## SN2 Reactivity of N-Heterocycles

Computational investigation of SN2 mechanisms and substituent effects using DFT calculations.
